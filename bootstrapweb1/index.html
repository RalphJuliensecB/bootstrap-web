Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg is an American business executive, author, and philanthropist, best known for her role as the Chief Operating Officer (COO) of Facebook (now Meta Platforms, Inc.). Born on August 28, 1969, in Washington, D.C., Sandberg graduated summa cum laude from Harvard University with a bachelor's degree in economics. She later earned a Master of Business Administration from Harvard Business School.
